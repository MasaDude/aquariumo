Aquariums

Aquariums are a good view for everyone to have
in his house, his work office, etc... You can simply enjoy just with watching



Let's show you some of their types and the suitable
conditions, the requirements for each of them
(I'll classify according to their sizes)
:

1) (500-700 L)




Those require a good filteration system and to fill the aquarium itself
with media, fish, decoration parts so you can not see this huge aquarium looks empty.



2) (150-400 L)




They also require a good filteration system but it could be smaller than
the first one, they would be much better if you filled them with
sea or slat water and surely seafish and sea environment



3) (100-40 L)




They also require a good filteration system but not as the previous ones.
Spongy filter with a good size is very good and efficient you can also put a powerhead filter.
Fill them with aquatic plants and enjoy I find this size as
the most suitable size for aquatic plants.



4) (20-60 L)




They are pretty good with aquatic plants perfect perfect.
I would prefer Betta fish to put and enjoy this one.



About aquariums

Each aquarium needs a proper treatment before you buy one,
you must search and choose the suitable size.
After choosing your aquarium you must find fish type that fits
your aquarium size. After that you can search and choose the
aquarium environment that fits with your fish type it's
a very important point. Then, you have to wait so the Biological
period takes place and then you can make a good environment for your fish.
It's important to choose a good filteration system, good nutrition
and good water.
